Shrimp Recipe

A delicious shrimp recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 cup chicken stock
  • 1 tablespoon reduced-sodium soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch
  • 1 tablespoon minced garlic
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste
  • 3 tablespoons sesame oil
  • 1 (16 ounce) package frozen stir-fry vegetables
  • 20 uncooked medium shrimp, peeled and deveined

Steps

    Step 1

  1. Gather all ingredients.

    2. Step 2

  2. Mix chicken stock, soy sauce, cornstarch, and garlic in a bowl; season with salt and pepper.

    3. Step 3

  3. Heat sesame oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat until oil shimmers;
    cook and stir vegetables in hot oil until softened, about 4 minutes.

    4. Step 4

  4. Add shrimp; cook and stir until shrimp begin to turn pink, about 3 minutes.

    5. Step 5

  5. Stir chicken stock mixture into vegetable-shrimp mixture.
    Continue to cook and stir until vegetables and shrimp
    are coated and sauce is thickened, about 5 minutes more.
